Welcome to Eventi.

Eventi. is a simple, yet robust, event planning website. Eventi. has been developed as a display of the skills learned by the group, with goals of creating a fluid event planning website to be used by individuals looking to keep track of some of the events in their lives; such as going out for dinner with a group of friends, noting the time and place of a family gathering, and scheduling meetings with fellow employees, just to name a few. We hope that you can find Eventi. useful in some way, and that our time and effort that has been put into this project shows in our work.
